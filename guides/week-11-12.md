## ðŸŽ“ Training Focus Areas

### Command Development
**Sit Command:**
- Use high-value treats
- Practice in different locations
- Add duration (3-5 seconds)
- Combine with name recognition

**Stay Command:**
- Start with 1-2 seconds
- Gradually increase duration
- Practice with distractions
- Always release with "okay"

**Come Command:**
- Use in secure, enclosed areas only
- Make it the most rewarding experience
- Never call for something unpleasant
- Practice multiple times daily

### Leash Introduction
- **Week 11:** Indoor leash wearing (5-10 minutes)
- **Week 12:** Indoor walking practice
- **Goal:** Comfortable with leash before boarding

## ðŸŒ Socialization Protocol

### Safe Exposure Guidelines
- **Vaccinated Dogs Only** - Until fully vaccinated
- **Controlled Environments** - Avoid dog parks and public areas
- **Positive Associations** - Treats and praise for calm behavior
- **Short Sessions** - 10-15 minutes maximum

### Weekly Socialization Goals
**Week 11:**
- Meet 3-5 new people
- Experience 2-3 new sounds
- Explore 1-2 new textures
- Supervised play with 1 vaccinated dog

**Week 12:**
- Meet 5-7 new people
- Car ride practice (short trips)
- Different floor surfaces
- Grooming introduction (brushing)

## ðŸš¨ Common Challenges

### Teething Behavior
- **Symptoms:** Increased chewing, mild discomfort
- **Solutions:** Appropriate chew toys, frozen treats
- **Avoid:** Punishment for natural behavior

### Vaccination Reactions
- **Monitor:** Energy levels, appetite, injection site
- **Normal:** Mild lethargy for 24-48 hours
- **Concerning:** Severe reactions, contact vet immediately

### Socialization Overwhelm
- **Signs:** Excessive panting, hiding, trembling
- **Response:** Remove from situation, provide comfort
- **Prevention:** Shorter sessions, positive associations
