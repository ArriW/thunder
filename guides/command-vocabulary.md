# 📚 Command & Marker Vocabulary

Consistency in language accelerates your puppy's learning curve. Use the **exact** words below across all handlers and settings so Thunder never has to guess what you mean.

---
## 🔊 Marker Words
| Category | Word | Purpose |
|----------|------|---------|
| Primary Reward Marker | **Yes** | Instant feedback ‑ a treat follows every time |
| Secondary/Event Marker | **Good** | Maintains behaviour; reward may follow intermittently |
| Release/Reset | **Okay** | Signals the exercise is finished |

> ⚡ *Why not "Good boy"?* – Single-word, crisp markers are clearer and faster to deliver.

---
## 🐾 Core Obedience Cues
| Behaviour | Cue Word | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Eye Contact | **Focus** | Replaces generic "Look" – preferred by handler |
| Name Response | **Thunder** | Acts as an attention primer before another cue |
| Sit | **Sit** | Hind-end on floor until released |
| Down | **Down** | Elbows & hips on ground; stay until released |
| Stay | **Stay** | Hold current position; optionally add duration/distance |
| Come | **Here** | High-value recall cue; always reward lavishly |
| Leave It | **Leave** | Disengage from object or distraction |
| Heel | **Close** | Walk politely at left knee |

---
## 🛏️ Stationing & Environmental Cues
| Behaviour | Cue Word | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Go to Crate | **Den** | Preferred over "Crate" to keep tone calm |
| Bed/Mat Settle | **Relax** | Formerly "Place" – redundant, replaced by clearer Relax cue |
| Potty | **Go Potty** | Used only in designated toilet area |
